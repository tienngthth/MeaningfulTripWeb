I. INTRODUCTION
The website is designed for a volunteering organization Meaningful Trip Beautiful You. The website has 5 pages including Home, Projects Information, Selection Process, Get Ready and Contact Us. All the pages cover sufficient information with images and texts including organization information, project information, several forms for queris, messages and subscription. The website is designed to be interactive and responsive with appropriate and lively animations using purely HTM, Javascript (window methods, time events, local storage, ...) and CSS (normal styling techniques, media and grid for responsive, transition and keyframes for animation, ...). 
Chrome is used to test the full operation of the website.

II. FEATURES
1. Navigation bar stick to the top of every pages.
+ Drop-down buttons, buttons and links lead to 1 of the 5 pages, google map website or hotline form.
+ Responsive: Navigation bar becomes a menu that can be expand or collapse by clicking an icon at the top-left corner when the screen gets smaller.
+ Once openning the menu or the hotline form, user can not do anything else. Click anywhere outside these two panes or click to the menu/close icon will close the panes.
2. Messenger and to-top-page buttons staying at the bottom-right corner of every pages.
+ The messenger button is animated. It reacts once and stops moving when being hovered.
+ The to-top-page button will appear when the user scroll down the page.
+ The two buttons stay at the front, overlapse the page's content.
3. Footer for all the pages except Contact Us
+ Subcribe form, meadia links, 4-column general information (the last column is the facebook plugin). 
+ Headers of these columns are links that lead to 1 of the pages.
+ Project names under Join Us section are links lead to Projects Information, the Projects Information page after loaded is auto scrolled down to the selected project information part.
4. 4 forms in total (subsribe form in the footer and in the Contact Us page, message form and hotline form). 
+ Once the submit button of a form is pressed, that form is checked and the webpage displays an alert if any required field is empty or displays an successful announcement.
5. Slider in the home page
+ Auto switches page after 3.5s. 
+ The slider and the quote section next to it also responses when being hovered.
+ The sliders stop switching when being hovered and start auto switching again when not being hovered anymore.
6. "Ask for more" link in the Project Information page
+ Leads to message form in the Contact Us page, it auto generates the subject for the form according to the project where the link is clicked.
7. Project image in the Project Information page
+ Changes when hovered, are links that leads to the Selection Process page.
8. Sections column in the Get Ready page
+ All sections can be expand or collapse by seperate buttons or expand all and collapse all buttons.
9. Contact Us page
+ Google map plug in
10. There are a lot of links and buttons in the website. Some open the new page in the same tab, some open a new tab. 
11. Whenever user the page is reloaded, its scrolled up to the top.
