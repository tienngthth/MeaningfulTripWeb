Who can apply?

To qualify for an exchange to one of our projects, an applicant must have/be:

  • Worldwide youths that are between 18 and 25 years old
  • A minimum English language level of IELTS 5.5 or equivalent
  • An valid application must be at least 1 months before the project start date

What will I do joing the program?

You'll need to research our main projects and identify the type of project you wish to partipate.
Please note we have many sub-projects for each main project that have similar job description and other information.

How do I apply

Video round

Step 1 Prepare a 2-5 minutes video answering these questions:

  • Q1: Introduce yourself (your name, your age, your work/study, your interests,..)
  • Q2: What do you know about this project?
  • Q3: How can you contribute to this project? Explain in terms of your 1 strength and 1 weakness.
  • Q4: Tell us about your worst working experience and how did you overcome it?

Step 2Send your video to our email: beautifultrip@gmail.com
*Your email should follow the format for the subtitle as: [your name][project name][start date of the project]

Step 3 Check your email box frequently to recevie the video result within 7 working days and further instructions.

Interview round after passing video round (optional)

You may be asked for face to face interview depending on your applied projects.
Setting interview instructions will be sent together with your video result.

Get your result and expectation setting meeting (ESM) announcement

It may take from 7 (projects require video only) to 14 working days to get your final result.
But we still need one last meeting in order to set expectation for you regarding the working and living conditions and the responsibilities from all parties.
Setting ESM instructions will be sent together with your final result.
* Meeting highlights:

  • Set expectations
  • Digital signs or physical signs for the contract

Frequently asked questions

When do I have to pay the fee?

When you arrive at your volunteer place, you can hand in the fee to the project manager there.

Can I apply for the project if I am 1 or 2 months to 18 years old?

You can apply for the project as long as you are 18 years old before the project start date.

Can I adjust the job description and have days off?

You can discuss with the project manager about job description and days off policy in the ESM day and during the project time as well.

Where can I find the full list of projects and get more information?

The list will be sent together with your video result.

Can I apply to multiple projects at the same time?

Yes, you can. But you can just participate in one project per time. You can select one project to join among all the accepted projects. But the decision has to be made 1 month prior the project start date.

Do I have to make multiple videos for multiple projects?

Yes, you do.

What should I use to record my video? Is there any requirement regarding the video quality? Do I have to edit the video?

Please make sure the video has acceptable qualification in terms of sounds and views. No editing work required.
