What are the Sustainable Development Goals?

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), also known as the Global Goals, were adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015 as a universal call to action to end poverty, protect the planet and ensure that all people enjoy peace and prosperity by 2030.

What is Meaningful Trip's role?

Meaningful Trip is an international organization that creates opportunies for youth and our partners to contribute to the SGD. By that, activating leaderships and strive for a better world.
Our projects are designed to serve SDG 4, 11 and 15.

Waste To Love

Description

Households are the main contributor to food wastage in Malaysia. Its food waste occupy most of the landfill. This is caused by the attitude, lack of awareness and actions of the general public towards food wastage. We aim to increase awareness, change attitude and initiate easy & obvious actions of youth towards reducing preventable food waste & fully utilizing food waste for them to influence their peers and their households.
