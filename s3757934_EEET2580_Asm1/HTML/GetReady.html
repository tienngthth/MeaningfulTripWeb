Come to Vietnam on volunteer

Wondering about what to prepare? Here are the basic information that you may find helpful! For more information, contact us!

Please prepare a 3-month single tourist visa. Visa guideline with our partner agency information will be sent after you get accepted.

Health insurance and travel insurance are required for all volunteer.
Project fee of $120 for MTBU operation and events will be collected by the project manager when you arrive at the volunteer place. Please prepare exactly 120 US Dollars. Please prepare around 200000 VND for transportation from the airport to your accommodation.

We do not cover any expenses including flights, insurance, .... Accommodation and meals are provided in some projects.

Costs of living for 45 days in Vietnam (Vietnamese Dong)

Accommodation 3000000 - 6000000 VND
Meals 1000000 VND - 5000000 VND
Incidential Fees 1000000 VND - 2000000 VND
Personal Expenses (launtry, transportation, entertainment, ...) 1000000 VND - 5000000 VND
Transportation (Bus, Grab, Taxi, ...) 500000 VND - 2000000 VND
Total 6500000 VND - 20000000 VND
For project that doesn't provide and cover accommodation. You can consider one of our hostel partners, $5/day with breakfast covered. We do recommend all the volunteer to live together. Its a great chance to make new friends while doing such meaningful activities together.
  1. Preview all the given materials save time for an ESM. Via the meeting, you can discuss with the project manager about your job descriptions, living conditions and any other concerns.
  2. Confirm for housing decision. We can help you to book the hostel for the day you come.
  3. Apply for visa and buy health insurance. Our provided visa guidline may be helpful!
  4. Don't forget to prepare exactly 120 US Dollars for project fee and exchange some Vietnamese Dong. You will need VND for transportation at the airport.
  5. Prepare youselves with Vietnam context, difficulties and culture.
  6. Don't hestitate to ask us for any concerns!
One of our volunteer will come and pick you at the airport. Your new friend will help you to buy sim card and exchange money if needed.
